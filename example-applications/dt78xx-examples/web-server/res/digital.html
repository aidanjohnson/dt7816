Digital Input

There are 8 general purpose digital input lines in the I/O connector. These pins may also be used for other purposes depending on the capabilities of the board, such as, external clock inputs, external trigger inputs, counter/timer inputs or phase/measure counter inputs. The inputs are +5V tolerant and 22k Ohm pull-ups are provided.
